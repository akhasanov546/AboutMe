Why do I like to bike?


I like to bike because it calms me down after a stressful day. Biking is a repetitive motion, which is helpful when I'm anxious about a test or homework. Also, I enjoy biking because it is a healthy activity to do instead of sitting home and watching videos. Finally, I like to bike because I can see amazing areas like different parks or neighborhoods.
